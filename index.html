Rules and Regulations

ON CAMPUS RULES AND REGULATIONS:

1. Consumption of alcohol, drugs, smoking etc… is strictly prohibited.

2. Entry of unregistered vehicles in the campus is prohibited.

3. Rash driving on campus is an offence.

4. Attempts to damage Institute property will be penalized strictly.

5. Any instance of unseemly behavior or of activities indicating lack of integrity and honesty on the part of the students will be regarded as a breach of discipline.

 

CONTEST RULES AND REGULATIONS:

1. The product  should be a mobile/web application or any other software.(IOT hardware also can be made for smart city project)

2. Team size should be 5.

3. Only students are allowed to register as participants. Professionals can register themselves as mentors.

4. No plagiarism will be tolerated in any form whatsoever.(plagiarism in the sense ? )

5. Final Submission will consist of the .apk file for Android, .xap or .appx for Windows, and .ipa for iOS app. (.jar .py )

6. Accommodation (space to rest during the constant 36 hours contest), lunch/dinner and internet connectivity will be provided during the event hours.

7. Participants will have to bring their own laptops and chargers.

8. No apks, software installations, etc. would be provided from the event team
